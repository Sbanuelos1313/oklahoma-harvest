How It Works

Simple as a farmers market
from your phone.

01
📍
Find by Location

Enter your city, ZIP code, or use your GPS. Set your radius and discover producers you never knew were this close.

02
🛒
Browse & Reserve

See live inventory — what's available today. Single-producer carts keep it simple. No surprises at pickup.

03
💳
Pay Securely

All payments happen in-app by card. No cash exchanges. Producers get paid directly through their own Stripe account.

04
🌾
Pickup or Delivery

Choose pickup at the farm, at a local drop point, or select delivery. Real food, real people, from our place to yours.
